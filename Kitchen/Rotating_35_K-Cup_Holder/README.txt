# Summary

Rotating 35 K-Cup Holder that is designed to snap together firmly without the need for glue.
You will need to print:
1x Base
1x Carousel Top
1x Carousel Bottom
14x Carousel Bar
28x K-cup Holder for bottom 4 rows
7x K-cup Holder for top row

It is very important that you orient the vertical carousel bars correctly (see pictures) 

Please note that this item was designed to be printed in PLA. The pieces were made to snap together firmly after cooling. It may work with other materials but significant shrinking or expanding may require sanding or glue afterwards. 



# Print Settings

Printer: Felix 3.1
Rafts: No
Supports: No

Notes: 
To minimize wobbling print the base with a high infill and wall thickness.
