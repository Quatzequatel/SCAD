# Summary

Here is a 2 piece case for keeping the pairs of bobbin and thread together. No more searching the box for the compliment color. Once closed, you can shake the case and all stays put. case will also stay closed by itself. I printed this with a 10% infill and 3 shells at .3 mm layer with a .4 mm nozzle.
